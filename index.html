Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously and this extension itself does not use your information in any way other than to achieve its stated purpose. (Hopefully, what those things are is self-explanatory.) Further, the developers and contributors to this extension never recieve any information from it.

To clarify, the purpose of this extension is that the Thunderbird application itself – as well as Google's services – maintains (if this extension works, identical copies of) your address book and your contacts. The privacy policies of both Thunderbird and Google (the only entities this extension talks to) apply here and we have no control over (nor take any responsibility for) their privacy practices.

The configuration settings you have set for this extension, your Google login info, and your contacts comprise the only information handled by this extension. These are stored locally on your computer, as well as, where necessary, exchanged with Google.
